The purpose

The purpose of this product is to reduce waste in the world, help the environment and find a product that is not harmful to health. One of the reasons why I chose to make this product is that the dye companies are using chemicals that if you use them too often can harm your health and another reason is for the environment because so many people throw garbage in this case. onion skins are accumulating and that is starting to be bad for the environment

The production

The hair dye we are doing is natural and does not harm your health or the environment In addition, in order not to generate more waste to the environment, we are going to sell chopped onions

Crops

The onion crop is established in areas with temperate and warm climates with low relative humidity. The onion grows mainly between 15 and 30 degrees of temperature, with its optimum temperature being 25 degrees. The optimal germination temperature is between 5 and 8 degrees, although once it has established it resists late frosts well. It is recommended to establish the onion crop in the spring and summer months, depending on our geographical area. This is a medium-cycle crop, so it will take 3 to 5 months from planting to have the onions ready to harvest, depending on the variety chosen. To plant onions you must bury the seed at a depth of 1-2 cm.
